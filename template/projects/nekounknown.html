NekoUnknown's Battlegrounds

NekoUnknown's Battlegrounds is a third person online arena shooter made with Unity. The game is based on Free for All matches, where the player has to defeat opponents and the first player to reach 20 kills wins the match. During the match, the player can pick up different types of weapons like an Axe, Mage Staff or Sniper to destroy the other players. There's also hats to pick up, which give the player a special skill to use in combat like invisibility, a bomb or a damage buff.


This game was made with a group of 4, using Unity and PUN(Photon Unity Networking) for the subject "Networks and Online Games" of the Bachelor's Degree in Video Game Design and Development at the UPC.

The game was really challenging to make as it was the first shooter I've ever worked and the result was a blast to play with friends!

My Contribution

Weapons

As a Gameplay Programmer, I implemented the weapon structure and the behaviour of each, making it so we could easily create different kinds of weapons by changing a couple of settings only. Made all the weapons the game currently has.

  • Pistol: Starter weapon, shoots fast but deals very low damage
  • Axe: Slow shooting weapon with a short range that deals tremendous damage, acts like a shotgun type weapon without spread.
  • Sniper: A very slow shooting weapon but with very high range and damage.
  • Mage Staff: Medium range weapon which shoots at a nice rate, acts like the automatic rifle of this game

Hats

I implemented the structure and behaviour of all the different hats of the game and their interactions with the weapons.

The different hats consist of:

  • Ninja Hat: Turns the player invisible and applies a damage buff to the next shot done while invisible.
  • Berserker Hat: Enrages the player, making him attack faster and deal more damage with his current weapon, it also makes the projectiles of said weapon return to the player.
  • Bomberman Hat: Gives the ability to deploy bombs and this bombs can be kicked to push them into enemies for deadly explosions.

Network & Others

In relation to the network, I implemented the synchronization of the player, which includes it's health, weapon and hat equipped and also the position of the weapon in the world, etc.

Also, worked on the shot registration and the synchronization of the boxes and crates that spawn weapons on the map.

I've also implemented the ragdoll effect which is triggered on death of a player and helped with the player aiming and shooting behaviour.
